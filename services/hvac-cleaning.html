More Than Just Duct Cleaning

Your HVAC system is more than ductwork. The air handler, evaporator coil, blower motor, drain pan, and return plenums all collect dust, moisture, and biological growth over time. If these components are not cleaned, even the best duct cleaning leaves contaminants circulating through your home.

Our HVAC cleaning service addresses every component in the air path, ensuring your entire system operates at peak performance while delivering the cleanest possible air.

What We Clean

  • Evaporator Coil - Removes dirt, mold, and buildup that reduce cooling capacity and create odors
  • Blower Motor & Fan - Cleans blades and housing for optimal airflow and reduced noise
  • Drain Pan & Lines - Prevents clogs that cause water damage and mold growth
  • Air Handler Cabinet - Removes accumulated dust and debris from the interior
  • Return & Supply Plenums - Cleans the main air distribution chambers
  • All Supply & Return Ducts - Complete ductwork cleaning with professional equipment
  • Vent Registers & Grilles - Cleaned and reinstalled

Benefits of Complete HVAC Cleaning

  • Lower Energy Bills - A clean system runs more efficiently, reducing energy consumption by up to 20%
  • Extended Equipment Life - Reduced strain on components means fewer repairs and longer system lifespan
  • Better Air Quality - Removing contaminants from the source eliminates recirculation
  • Fewer Breakdowns - Clean systems are less prone to mechanical failure
  • Even Temperature - Improved airflow means consistent comfort in every room
  • Odor Elimination - Removes the source of musty, stale HVAC smells

When to Schedule HVAC Cleaning

  • Every 2 to 3 years as routine maintenance
  • Before the start of heating or cooling season
  • After renovations or construction in your home
  • When you notice reduced airflow or uneven temperatures
  • If you see dust or debris around vent registers
  • When energy bills increase without explanation
