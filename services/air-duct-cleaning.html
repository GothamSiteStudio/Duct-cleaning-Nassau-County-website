Professional Air Duct Cleaning for Healthier Indoor Air

Your home's air ducts are the lungs of your HVAC system. Over time, they accumulate dust, dirt, pet dander, mold spores, pollen, and other contaminants that get circulated throughout every room each time your heating or cooling system runs.

Our professional air duct cleaning service removes these harmful contaminants, improving your indoor air quality and helping your family breathe easier. Whether you are dealing with allergies, asthma, visible dust around vents, or simply want a cleaner home, our service delivers noticeable results.

Our Air Duct Cleaning Process

  • Thorough Inspection - We start with a complete inspection of your HVAC system, checking all supply and return ducts, registers, and the air handler.
  • Source Removal Cleaning - Using the advanced RamAir International system, we dislodge and remove buildup from every duct in your home without causing damage to your ductwork.
  • HEPA-Filtered Collection - Powerful Nikro negative-air machines with HEPA filters capture all loosened debris, preventing it from entering your home.
  • Sanitization (Optional) - We can apply Benefect botanical disinfectant to eliminate remaining mold and bacteria. 100% non-toxic and safe for children and pets.
  • Post-Cleaning Verification - We verify that all ducts are clean and airflow is optimal before completing the job.

Signs Your Air Ducts Need Cleaning

  • Visible dust or debris around vent openings
  • Musty or stale odors when the HVAC system runs
  • Increased allergy or asthma symptoms indoors
  • Inconsistent airflow between rooms
  • Higher than normal energy bills
  • Recent home renovation or construction
  • New pet in the household
  • Visible mold growth near ducts or vents
