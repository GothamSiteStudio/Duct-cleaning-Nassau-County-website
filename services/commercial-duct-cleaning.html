Clean Air for Your Business

Commercial buildings accumulate dust, allergens, and contaminants faster than residential homes. Higher occupancy, heavier HVAC usage, and commercial cooking environments put constant strain on ductwork. Without regular cleaning, indoor air quality drops, energy costs rise, and health complaints increase.

Our commercial duct cleaning services are designed for the unique demands of business environments. We use industrial-grade equipment and work around your schedule to minimize disruption to your operations.

Industries We Serve

  • Office Buildings - Clean air supports employee productivity and reduces sick days
  • Restaurants & Kitchens - Remove grease buildup, cooking odors, and fire hazards from kitchen exhaust and HVAC
  • Retail Stores - Better air quality means a better customer experience
  • Medical & Dental Offices - Critical for patient safety and regulatory compliance
  • Schools & Daycares - Protect children and staff from airborne contaminants
  • Property Management - Multi-unit buildings, common areas, and shared HVAC systems
  • Houses of Worship - Clean air for large indoor gatherings

Our Commercial Process

  1. Site Assessment - We evaluate your HVAC system, ductwork layout, and specific concerns
  2. Custom Plan - We develop a cleaning plan tailored to your building and schedule
  3. Professional Cleaning - Industrial-strength source removal cleaning with HEPA filtration
  4. Sanitization (Optional) - Benefect botanical disinfectant for added protection
  5. Documentation - Before and after photos plus a detailed completion report

Why Businesses Choose Us

  • Flexible Scheduling - Nights, weekends, and off-hours available to avoid business disruption
  • Licensed & Insured - HIC #0660487 with full liability coverage for commercial properties
  • Industrial Equipment - RamAir and Nikro commercial-grade systems rated for large duct runs
  • Documentation - Detailed reports for property managers, landlords, and compliance records
  • Maintenance Plans - Recurring service agreements for consistent air quality year-round

Compliance & Health Standards

Regular duct cleaning helps commercial properties meet indoor air quality standards and pass health inspections. Restaurants and food service businesses are especially subject to HVAC cleanliness requirements. We provide documentation that can be used for inspections and compliance records.
