Why Sanitize Your Air Ducts?

Even after a thorough duct cleaning, microscopic mold spores, bacteria, and allergens can remain embedded on duct surfaces. Our sanitization and disinfection service eliminates these hidden contaminants, giving your family truly clean air to breathe.

We use Benefect botanical disinfectant, a Health Canada and EPA-registered product made from thyme oil. Unlike harsh chemical treatments, Benefect is 100% non-toxic and requires no rinsing, ventilation, or wait time. It is safe for children, pets, and sensitive individuals immediately after application.

About Benefect Botanical Disinfectant

  • 100% Botanical - Made from thyme oil (Thymol), with no synthetic chemicals
  • Hospital-Grade - Kills 99.9% of bacteria, mold, and fungi on contact
  • No Harsh Fumes - Safe to breathe during and after application
  • No Rinse Required - No post-treatment cleanup needed
  • Safe for Families - Non-toxic to children, pets, and allergy sufferers
  • EPA Registered - Meets strict regulatory standards for disinfection

When to Consider Sanitization

  • After professional air duct cleaning (recommended complement)
  • Visible mold detected inside ductwork
  • Musty or stale odors coming from vents
  • After water damage or flooding
  • Family members with allergies, asthma, or respiratory conditions
  • Moving into a new home
  • After construction or renovation

Our Sanitization Process

  1. Full Duct Cleaning - We remove all dust, debris, and visible contaminants first
  2. Inspection - We assess ductwork for mold, moisture, and problem areas
  3. Application - Benefect botanical disinfectant is applied throughout the entire duct system using professional foggers
  4. Air Flow Activation - The HVAC system distributes the treatment for complete coverage
  5. Verification - We confirm full coverage and explain the results

Sanitization is most effective when performed immediately after a professional duct cleaning. We recommend bundling both services for the best results and value.
