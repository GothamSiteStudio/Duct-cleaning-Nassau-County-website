Your Health Matters

Why Clean Your Air Ducts?

It is important to keep you, the next generation, and your home healthy. Are you or your family suffering from any of these?

🪠

Asthma & Allergies

Dirty ducts circulate allergens and irritants that trigger asthma attacks and allergic reactions throughout your home.

🦠

Dust Mites

Millions of dust mites live in contaminated ductwork, spreading waste particles that cause respiratory issues.

😷

Mold & Bacteria

Moisture in ductwork creates breeding grounds for mold and harmful bacteria that get blown into every room.

🐶

Pet Dander & Hair

Pet dander and hair accumulate in your ducts and recirculate continuously, affecting everyone in the household.

💨

Bad Odors

Musty smells from your vents indicate buildup of dust, debris, and potentially mold inside your duct system.

🌡️

High Energy Bills

Clogged ducts force your HVAC system to work harder, increasing energy consumption and utility costs significantly.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my air ducts cleaned?

The National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) recommends cleaning your air ducts every 3 to 5 years. However, homes with pets, smokers, recent renovations, or allergy sufferers may benefit from more frequent cleaning. If you notice dust buildup around vents, musty odors, or increased allergy symptoms, it is time to schedule a cleaning.

How much does air duct cleaning cost in Nassau County?

The cost varies based on the size of your home, number of vents, and the complexity of your HVAC system. We offer free in-home estimates so you always know exactly what to expect before we begin. Call us at (516) 324-8078 for your free quote.

Is your sanitization safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. We use Benefect, a 100% botanical, non-toxic disinfectant that eliminates 99.9% of mold and bacteria. Unlike chemical-based products, Benefect is derived from natural botanical extracts and is completely safe for families, children, pets, and anyone with chemical sensitivities.

How long does air duct cleaning take?

A standard residential air duct cleaning typically takes between 3 to 5 hours, depending on the size of your home and the number of vents. Our team uses the advanced RamAir system that works efficiently while ensuring thorough, damage-free cleaning of your entire duct system.

Do you service all of Nassau County?

Yes! We provide air duct cleaning services throughout all of Nassau County, including Hempstead, Freeport, Garden City, Great Neck, Levittown, Oceanside, Mineola, Valley Stream, Syosset, Jericho, Massapequa, and all surrounding communities. No matter where you are in Nassau County, we can help.

What signs indicate I need duct cleaning?

Common signs include: visible dust or debris around vent openings, musty or stale odors when the HVAC runs, increased allergy or asthma symptoms indoors, inconsistent airflow between rooms, higher than normal energy bills, and visible mold growth near ducts or vents. If you notice any of these, call us for a free inspection.
