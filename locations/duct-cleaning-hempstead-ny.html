Trusted Air Duct Cleaning Services in Hempstead

Hempstead is one of the largest and most diverse communities in all of Nassau County, with a rich housing stock that spans decades of construction. Many homes in Hempstead were built during the 1940s through the 1960s, and a significant number still have their original ductwork in place. Over time, these aging systems accumulate dust, allergens, mold spores, and other contaminants that can affect your family's health and your HVAC system's efficiency.

At Duct Cleaning Nassau County, we provide thorough, professional air duct cleaning to Hempstead residents using advanced source-removal techniques. Whether you live near Hempstead Lake State Park, along the village's tree-lined streets, or in one of the many apartment complexes throughout the area, our licensed technicians deliver results you can feel with every breath. We understand the unique challenges that come with older Hempstead homes, including galvanized steel ductwork, original furnace connections, and limited access points.

Our team is fully licensed (HIC #0660487) and insured, and we proudly serve all neighborhoods within Hempstead and the surrounding areas. From single-family homes to multi-unit buildings, we bring the same dedication to every job. Our Services in Hempstead

  • Air Duct Cleaning: Complete source-removal cleaning of all supply and return ducts, registers, and grilles throughout your Hempstead home.

  • UV Light Installation: Germicidal UV-C lights installed in your HVAC system to continuously kill mold, bacteria, and viruses.
