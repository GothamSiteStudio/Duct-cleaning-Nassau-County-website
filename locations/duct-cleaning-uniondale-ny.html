Trusted Air Duct Cleaning Services in Uniondale

Uniondale is a diverse, centrally located community in Nassau County known for being home to the Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum and the surrounding entertainment and commercial district. This vibrant neighborhood features a mix of residential streets lined with older homes, many built during the 1940s through the 1960s, alongside newer developments and commercial properties. With an older housing stock, many Uniondale homes have ductwork that has been in service for decades without ever receiving professional cleaning.

At Duct Cleaning Nassau County, we provide Uniondale homeowners and businesses with thorough, professional air duct cleaning to improve indoor air quality and HVAC efficiency. Over the years, dust, pet dander, pollen, mold spores, and other pollutants build up inside your duct system and are recirculated every time your heating or air conditioning runs. Our trained technicians use powerful HEPA-filtered vacuum equipment and specialized agitation tools to remove every trace of contamination, giving you noticeably cleaner air from the moment we finish.

We are licensed (HIC #0660487), insured, and family-owned, and we believe in doing right by every customer. For Uniondale residents, that means free estimates, transparent pricing, and work we stand behind completely. Whether your home is a classic ranch near the Coliseum, a colonial on a quiet side street, or a Cape Cod that has been in your family for generations, we have the experience and equipment to get your ducts thoroughly clean.

Our Services in Uniondale

  • Air Duct Cleaning: Complete source-removal cleaning of all supply and return ducts, registers, and grilles throughout your Uniondale home.
  • Dryer Vent Cleaning: Professional lint removal to eliminate fire hazards and restore full dryer efficiency.
  • UV Light Installation: Germicidal UV-C lights installed in your HVAC system to continuously destroy mold, bacteria, and airborne pathogens.
  • Sanitization & Disinfection: Non-toxic botanical treatment that eliminates 99.9% of microbial contaminants from inside your ductwork.
