Our Story

Duct Cleaning Nassau County was founded with a simple mission: to help families in Nassau County breathe cleaner, healthier air. As a family-owned and operated business, we understand that your home is where your family feels safe and protected. That is why we treat every home we enter as if it were our own.

Our company is built on the belief that clean air should not be a luxury. Too many families suffer from allergies, asthma, and respiratory issues caused by contaminated air ducts without even knowing the source. We are here to change that, one home at a time.

We serve all communities across Nassau County, from Hempstead and Freeport to Great Neck and Garden City.

What sets us apart from large franchise operations is our personal commitment to every job. When you call us, you are talking to people who care about your indoor air quality and your family's well-being. We do not cut corners, use bait-and-switch pricing, or rush through jobs. We do it right.

Licensed, Insured, and Certified

We are fully licensed by the State of New York (HIC License #0660487) and carry comprehensive insurance. Every technician on our team is trained and certified in the latest air duct cleaning techniques and safety protocols.

Professional-Grade Equipment

We invest in the best equipment available so you get the best results. Our primary cleaning system is the RamAir International system, which is the fastest growing duct cleaning technology in the world.

  • RamAir International System - Advanced air whip technology that dislodges debris without damaging your ductwork. Keeps your home clean, air healthier, and ducts damage-free. Learn about our air duct cleaning process.
  • Nikro Industrial Equipment - Powerful HEPA-filtered negative air machines that capture and contain contaminants during the cleaning process.
  • Benefect Botanical Disinfectant - 100% non-toxic, plant-derived sanitizer that eliminates 99.9% of mold and bacteria. Safe for children, pets, and sensitive individuals. Learn about our sanitization services.
  • UV Light Systems - Hospital-grade ultraviolet light units that continuously kill mold, bacteria, and viruses inside your HVAC system for up to 13,000 hours. Explore UV light installation.
